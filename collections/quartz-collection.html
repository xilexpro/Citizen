Frequently Asked Questions

What does quartz mean on a watch?

A Watch whose battery-powered mechanism uses electrical energy to cause vibrations in a synthetic quartz crystal at a very high rate of 100 kilocycles per second.

Are quartz watches good?

Electronic Quartz watches are some of the most accurate watches ever produced.

How long will quartz watch last?

Quartz watches batteries are designed to last for a period between 2 to 3 years depending on the type of the watch the battery is used for. If the watch is a high-functional timepiece (like a chronograph timepiece) the energy consumption will be more and may last up to 2 years but if it is a simple three hands watch, then it may last as long as 3 years.

What is better quartz or automatic watches?

Both quartz and automatic watches are dependable and can tell time with reasonable accuracy. However, Quartz watches are considered to be more accurate. Mechanical watches are valued for their craftsmanship, artistry, and beauty. So, it’s unfair to say between quartz and automatic which movement is better, it depends on the buyer if he/she is buying a watch just for its accuracy or for the craftsmanship and artistry of the mechanical movement.
