Citizen Eco-Drive men's Watches

What is the ideal source of power for a watch? CITIZEN created the world's first light-powered analogue quartz watch in 1976. We named the technology Eco-Drive in acknowledgement of its eco-friendly origins. It converts any kind of light into energy to power a watch and stores the surplus on a power cell. On a full charge, an Eco-Drive watch runs for months even in darkness with no need for regular battery replacement. As a light-power pioneer, we continue exploring new possibilities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does a citizen eco drive have a battery?

Eco-Drive is a light-powered technology that drives watches by capturing light and converting it into energy using an inbuilt solar cell.

Are Eco drives good watches?

Eco-Drive is an eco-friendly technology that enables our watches to be powered by any light source (such as sunlight, indoor fluorescent lights, desk lights, candle lights etc.) continuously and sustainably, eliminating the need to replace watch batteries and prevents millions of watch batteries from entering landfills each year. A single full charge is enough to keep Eco-Drive working for over six months in total darkness. Indeed, some models equipped with power save function can run for as long as seven years.

What is the difference between solar and Eco-Drive watches?

Eco-Drive watches are powered by any light source whereas solar watches are charged only by sunlight.

Is solar watch better than an automatic watch?

We can describe mechanical watches as a manual car, where you must change gears to drive it, which some people do enjoy it a lot. But in terms of ease of use, a lot of people still prefer solar watches. So, it mainly depends on the wearer what he/she likes.
